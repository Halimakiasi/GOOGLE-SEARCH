The dog(canis familiaris)is a domesticated wolf. Also called domestic dog blong to kingdom animalia. Its derived from the extinct Pleistocene wolf. Dogs were the first species to be domesticated by hunter gatheres over 15,000 years ago, before development of agriculture. Due to their long association with human being, dogs have expanded to a large number of domestic individuals and gained ability to thrive on a starch rich diet that would be in-adequate for other canids.

The dogs has been selectively bred over millenia for various behaviours, sensory capabilities, and physically attributes. Dog breed vary widel in shape, size, and color. They perform many roles for humans, such as hunting, herding, pulling loads, protection, assisting police and millitary, companionship, therapy and aiding disable people.
